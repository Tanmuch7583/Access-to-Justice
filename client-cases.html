Available Legal Assistance Options

Unfair Work Dismissal

If you believe you've been unfairly dismissed from your job, we can connect you with legal experts who can review your case and advise on potential wrongful termination claims.

Sexual Assault/Harassment

Our network includes specialized lawyers who can provide confidential support and guidance for survivors of sexual assault or harassment, both in workplace and other settings.

Landlord-Tenant Dispute

Facing issues with your landlord? Whether it's unfair eviction, security deposit disputes, or maintenance problems, we can connect you with housing law specialists.

Discrimination at Workplace

If you've experienced discrimination based on gender, race, religion, disability, or other protected characteristics, our lawyers can help you understand your rights.

Wage Theft

Not being paid overtime, minimum wage violations, or unpaid wages? Our legal network can help you recover what you're owed.
