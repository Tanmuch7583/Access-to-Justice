Welcome to Access to Justice

The Access to Justice Centre at the University of Zimbabwe is a pioneering initiative that promotes access to justice through research, legal aid, and clinical legal education. By providing legal services, advocating for law reform, and empowering communities, the centre bridges the gap between the law and those who need it most.
